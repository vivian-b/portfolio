    Check It

    MANDATE

    Create an news aggregator app with UI/UX principles in mind.

    Researching the topic of news app and misinformation, the fictional app answers the issue of user engagement in regards to news information. It resulted in the making of a news aggregation app mainly tailored to millenials

    Check It aims to simplify the design and create a bias-free user experience for readers to easily digest news. By customizing news based on the user's interests, the mobile app provides a simple and effective solution to the overwhelming amount of information presented on various news platforms. With a focus on combating fake news, Check It offers a clear and concise approach to help young adults access and share news with confidence.
