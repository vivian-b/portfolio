    MANDATE

    Create a manual for the Graphic Design program at Dawson College in Montréal

    As type is one of the main themes of Graphic Design, the brochure is minimal and heavily focused on typography. To not take away the strong presence from the thick black type, the background is of a solid yellow colour to enhance the boldness of the letters.

    The brochure aims to present and promote Graphic Design at the school to those who are curious and interested in the program. Information includes the course list, student works and faculty list.
